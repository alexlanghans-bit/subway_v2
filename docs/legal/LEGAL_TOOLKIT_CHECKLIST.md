# Legal Toolkit — Checklist (Internal)
**Version:** 0.1 • **Date:** 2025-11-27

> Ensure contracts support the 12k-in-10 plan while remaining fair and enforceable.

## Franchise Agreements
- [ ] Site approval & spacing/density controls.
- [ ] Remodel obligations & timelines; non-compliance remedies.
- [ ] Data access & system participation (POS, KDS, inventory, loyalty).
- [ ] Transfer approvals & right of first refusal.
- [ ] Non-renewal terms linked to standards and economics.
- [ ] Portfolio actions: relocate, consolidate, close (with defined incentives).

## Programs & Incentives
- [ ] Voluntary exit packages (templates, sliding scales).
- [ ] Buy-fix-refranchise terms (JV, managed ops).
- [ ] Remodel incentive schedules (royalty relief, capex co-funding).
- [ ] Operator consolidation (M&A guardrails).

## Communications & Disclosure
- [ ] Clear operator memos; risk disclosures; timelines.
- [ ] Consistent national vs regional statements.
- [ ] Vendor/supplier amendments for spec changes.

## Compliance & QA
- [ ] Independent audit rights (food safety, brand standards).
- [ ] Data privacy & loyalty program compliance.
- [ ] ADA, labor, and wage-hour policy alignment.

> Coordinate with Finance on funding mechanisms and with Ops on enforceability realities.
