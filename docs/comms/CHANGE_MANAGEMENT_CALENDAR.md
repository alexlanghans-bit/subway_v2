# Change Management Calendar — Subway 2.0
**Version:** 0.1 • **Date:** 2025-11-27

## Objectives
- Predictable, transparent communication of the 12k-in-10 roadmap.
- Reduce uncertainty; keep operators and teams engaged with facts, actions, and help.

## 10-Year Cadence (Macro)
- **Quarterly:** System webcast (CEO/COO + Portfolio + Culinary + Tech).
- **Biannual:** Regional roadshows + operator councils.
- **Annual:** Public strategy letter; publish portfolio scorecards and Food OS roadmap.

## Artifacts
- Slides, FAQ, one-pagers, dashboards (read-only), case studies.
- Translation/localization where needed.

## Feedback & Monitoring
- Post-event surveys; operator council feedback loops.
- Social/media monitoring; rapid response templates.

## RACI
- **Accountable:** COO (execution), CCO/CMO (narrative), CFO (funding).
- **Responsible:** Portfolio Office, Comms, Culinary, Tech, Field Ops.
- **Consulted:** Legal, Supply Chain, HR.
- **Informed:** All operators, vendors, media as appropriate.
