# KPI Catalog — Subway 2.0
**Version:** 0.1 • **Date:** 2025-11-27

> Canonical list of metrics, owners, and definitions. Update as systems evolve.

## Economics
- **AUV** — Average Unit Volume (12-month trailing unless noted). Source: POS.
- **4-wall EBITDA %** — Store contribution after labor, COGS, occupancy. Source: Store P&L.
- **Labor %** — Labor cost / Net sales. Target by format & band.
- **Occupancy %** — Rent + CAM + taxes / Net sales.
- **COGS %** — Food + paper / Net sales.

## Demand & Mix
- **Transactions** — # of orders.
- **Average Check** — Net sales / transactions.
- **Off-premise %** — Delivery + takeout (excl. dine-in).
- **Digital mix** — 1P + 3P as % of sales.
- **1P vs 3P** — First-party vs third-party delivery share.

## Ops Quality
- **Order accuracy** — % correct orders (POS/guest feedback).
- **Speed (emake-to-exit)** — Seconds per order by channel.
- **Waste/shrink %** — Food waste / purchases.
- **Throughput** — Orders per 15-min at peak.

## Portfolio & Compliance
- **Store count by Tier** — 1/2/3 segmentation.
- **Remodel compliance %** — Stores meeting current image/equipment spec.
- **Closures/Relocations** — Rolling 12-month.
- **White-space progress** — # new sites opened against plan.

## People
- **Retention by role** — 90-day & 12-month.
- **Time-to-proficiency** — Days to certified.
- **Internal promotions** — Crew→Shift→AGM→GM→MU Lead.

## Brand/Guest
- **NPS / Satisfaction** — Survey scores.
- **Quality perception** — Tracker score.
- **Value perception** — Tracker score.

### Data Stewardship
Each KPI has: **Definition**, **Calculation**, **Owner**, **Source System**, **Update Cadence**, **QA Notes**.
