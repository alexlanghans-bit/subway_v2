# Supply Chain OS — TEMPLATE
**Version:** 0.1 • **Date:** 2025-11-27

## Strategy
- Fewer DC partners; KPI-based SLAs; visibility to store-level forecasts.
- Produce quality & freshness standards; local/regional sourcing where feasible.
- Menu-linked procurement (Food OS cadence aligned to supplier readiness).

## Design
- **Forecasting:** Demand-driven replenishment tied to POS/loyalty data.
- **Logistics:** Route optimization with off-premise demand peaks in mind.
- **Quality:** Independent QA audits; cold chain monitoring.
- **Cost:** Index-linked contracts; hedging policy for key commodities.

## KPIs
- Fill rate, OTIF, shrink, lead time variance, landed cost per case, audit scores.

## Playbooks
- Supplier onboarding; DC transitions; contingency & risk management.
